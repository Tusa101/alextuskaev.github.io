Architecture & Design

I think in diagrams before I think in code.

Before a single endpoint gets written, I sketch the system — bounded contexts, message flows, failure modes, fan-out paths. Good architecture is a conversation, and diagrams are how it gets had.

I lean on C4 models for system context and containers, UML for class and state machines, and sequence diagrams to nail down async choreography across services. Tools of choice: PlantUML, Structurizr, draw.io, Mermaid, Excalidraw.

That's why I'm equally open to System Architect / Solution Architect roles — designing the shape of a system is the part of the job I enjoy the most.

And I'm just as comfortable leading the team that builds it — mentoring engineers, running technical interviews, owning delivery and code-review culture, and translating architecture decisions into a roadmap the team actually wants to ship.
